About Us



The Street Store



The Street Store is an English brand with over 5 years of history. The products reach out to thousands of satisfied customers in more than 20 countries each year. Behind the brand is a young and motivated team that does not stop making improvements in the models of each subsequent collection with a single goal - to provide customers with the best possible sneakers at the most affordable price.
